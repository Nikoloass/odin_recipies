Description

Get an authentic taste of Italian cousine with this simple to follow recipe for spaghetti and meat sauce

ingredients

  • An 8-ounce package of spaghetti should make about eight servings
  • you'll need a pound of lean ground beef. you can use a mix of ground beef and ground turkey, if you prefer
  • A store-bought spaghetti sauce will be fine
  • A full stick of butter is required to prepare the pan and to add richness and flavor to the baked spaghetti
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream each 1/3 mixture
  • Your choice of cheese mixture

Preparation

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water, then drain and set aside

  2. Cook the beef on the stove and discard the grease. Transfer to a bowl and mix in the spaghetti sauce

  3. Prepare the Baking dish, the spread half of the noodles in the bottom.

  4. top with the mixture of cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream

  5. cover with remaining spaghetti and butter

  6. pour the beef mixture over the spaghetti and spread in an even layer

  7. bake for 30 mins, top with cheese, and continue baking until the cheese is melted
